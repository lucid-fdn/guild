# Code of Conduct

Guild is built for careful, ambitious people working on agent infrastructure.
We want the project to feel rigorous without becoming hostile.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Assume good intent, but clarify quickly when something is unsafe or incorrect.
- Keep issues and pull requests focused on concrete behavior, evidence, and tradeoffs.
- Credit people for useful ideas and implementation work.
- Treat security, privacy, and agent-safety concerns seriously.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information, secrets, or credentials.
- Dismissing safety or security reports without review.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions after maintainers redirect.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; block users; or restrict participation when needed to keep the project safe and productive.

If you need to report a conduct concern, contact the maintainers privately.
